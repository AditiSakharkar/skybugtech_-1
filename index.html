Why Nutrient-rich food ?

prioritizing nutrient-rich foods in your diet is essential for maintaining good health, preventing diseases, and promoting optimal growth and development throughout life. It's about providing your body with the necessary tools for it to function at its best.

To know more join us !!

Nutrient Rich Food

"nutrient-rich" refers to foods that are dense in essential nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates. These foods provide a high level of nutrition relative to their calorie content.

VEG

  • Nutrient Rich veg options
  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Avocado

NON-VEG

  • Nutrient Rich non-veg options
  • Salmon
  • Eggs
  • Oysters
  • Venison

MEDICINS

  • Nutrient Rich medicinal options
  • Tulsi (Holy Basil)
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil
  • Flaxseeds
